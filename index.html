Pop Music Transcription

Jonathan Yaffe, Ben Maman, Amit H. Bermano
Tel Aviv University
Paper Code Colab

Abstract

Automatic Music Transcription (AMT) is the process of converting audio recordings of music into written musical notations with minimal human intervention, enabling the transformation of sound into tangible, editable, and analyzable forms. Current AMT approaches are restricted to piano and (some) guitar recordings, due to difficult data collection. In this project we attempt the task of AMT for pop music. This task is hard due to data collection difficulty and also the wide variety of pop instruments and sounds. The main idea is to take a model that was trained mainly on classic music and fine tune it for pop music.
